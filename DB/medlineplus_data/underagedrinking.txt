주제: Underage Drinking

Alcohol is the most widely misused substance among America's youth. Drinking by young people has big health and safety risks. It is dangerous because it:Causes many deaths and injuriesCan lead to poor decisions about engaging in risky behavior, such as drinking and driving or unprotected sexIncreases the risk of physical and sexual assaultCan lead to other problems, such as trouble in schoolMay interfere with brain developmentIncreases the risk of alcohol problems later in lifeKids often begin drinking to look "cool" or fit in with their peers. Parents can help their kids avoid alcohol problems.  Open communication and conversations about drinking are important. So is being involved in your child's life. Get help for your child if you suspect a drinking problem.NIH: National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism
